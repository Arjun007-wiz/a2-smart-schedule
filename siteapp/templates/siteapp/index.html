Overview

This platform removes the chaos of planning hangouts, coordinating schedules, and figuring out who is free when. It is built for fun and social connection — so you spend more time playing and zero time herding cats in chat.

Instead of manual texts, calendar checks, and slow replies, users submit availability once. The system does the rest.

How it works

The platform acts as an intelligent scheduling assistant.

1. User input

  • Simple form for available time slots
  • Private, secure availability
  • Select games you want to play

2. AI scheduling engine

  • Analyzes all submitted schedules
  • Finds overlapping free time
  • Matches on availability, game prefs, headcount, and duration

3. Automatic sessions

  • When enough players align, a session is scheduled
  • Everyone is notified of confirmed times

4. Time standardization

  • Scheduling is handled in PST (Pacific) for consistency

Registration & onboarding

Users register with email. During onboarding they enter available slots (by day and time), pick games they care about, and build a profile that improves future matchmaking and scheduling accuracy.

System features

Game database

  • Duration (e.g. 30 minutes, 1 hour)
  • Minimum players required
  • Optional maximum player limit

Smart scheduling logic

  • Session only when enough players are free
  • Time slots must cover the full required duration

Admin controls

  • Add and update games
  • Adjust details and system-wide scheduling rules
